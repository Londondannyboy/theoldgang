# Story Structure

## Rough Shape

### Part 1 — The New Girl (Layers 1-2)
Sarah arrives. First case. The Old Woman. Colleagues' dismissal. Real-world impossibilities. Escalating anomalies. The custody scene. The blood test.

### Part 2 — The Old Unit (Layers 3-4)
Sarah banished. The old police unit. Old Gang confirmed real. Booth's map breakthrough. The phone scene. Father's files. Corrupt Policeman reveal. Community alliances. Diamond Anne. The Camberwell Man's story.

### Part 3 — The Buried Truth (Layers 5-6)
Old crimes in plain sight. School of Thieves. Workhouse parallel. The Camberwell Man's revelation and transformation. Alliance forms. Excavation reveals True Old Gang. The drill scene. Confrontation with institutional corruption. Sarah says goodbye to her father.
